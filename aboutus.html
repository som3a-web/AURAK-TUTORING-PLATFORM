About AURAK Peer Tutoring

Empowering students through peer-to-peer learning and academic support

Our Mission & Vision

Building a community of learners who support each other's academic journey

The AURAK Peer Tutoring Platform was established with a clear mission: to create an inclusive academic support system that empowers students to achieve their full potential.


We believe that learning is a collaborative process, and that students can greatly benefit from the guidance and support of their peers who have successfully navigated similar academic challenges.


Our vision is to foster a culture of mutual support and academic excellence at AURAK, where every student has access to the resources and guidance they need to succeed.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Collaboration

We believe in the power of working together to achieve academic success through shared knowledge and experiences.

Excellence

We are committed to maintaining high standards of academic support and ensuring quality in every tutoring session.

Community

We strive to build a supportive learning community where students feel empowered to help each other grow.

Accessibility

We ensure that peer tutoring services are free and accessible to all AURAK students regardless of their background.

Our Journey

How the AURAK Tutoring Platform evolved over time

2022

Program Inception

The peer tutoring program was conceptualized by Dr. Khouloud Joseph to address student academic support needs.

2023

Pilot Program

Launched a small-scale pilot program with 20 tutors supporting 5 core courses across different departments.

2024

Digital Platform

Developed and launched the online tutoring platform to streamline the matching and scheduling process.

2025

Full Implementation

Expanded to support 50+ courses with 150+ tutors, serving over 2,500 students across AURAK.
