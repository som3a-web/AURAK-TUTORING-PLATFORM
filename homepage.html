Do you need help understanding your course?

The AURAK Peer Tutoring program gives you an opportunity to get help from fellow students and ask questions related to course material. Our peer tutors are AURAK students just like you, who excelled in their courses and are recommended by faculty. They will guide, encourage, and support you in your learning process.


Peer Tutoring service is FREE to all AURAK students!

How It Works

1. Create your account using your AURAK email

2. Browse available tutors for your courses

3. Schedule a session at your convenience

4. Connect with your tutor and get the help you need
